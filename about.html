HEY EVERYONE

My name is Liliana, and I'm currently attentding Texas A&M Universiry - San Antonio as a senior. I'm majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Business Administration, and working as a student worker for my universities Web Team. While working and attending school, I study web development independently as well as training in Jiu Jitsu in my free time.

I also have other interets such as nutrition, reading, and architecure. I have found nutriton a big part of my daily life as I am a vegetarian. Going on 3 years now, I know it's been so long! Nonetheless, I have never felt better physically and mentally.

My health will continue to be a significant importance in my life, as well as reading. In my younger years before I graduated high school, I had no interest in reading a single book. That was until I had found out the books I was reading were not the right ones, at least for my taste. So I decided to branch out and I found a huge liking to science, social networking, and business books. My most recent books I've read so far are Never Eat Alone by Keith Ferrazzi, Biography of Elon Musk, and Atomic Habits by James Clear.
