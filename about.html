HEY EVERYONE

Hello, I'm Liliana, a senior at Texas A&M University - San Antonio, pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Business Administration. Alongside my studies, I'm a student worker on the university's Web Team, which allows me to further explore web development with my teams and independently. In addition to my academic and professional commitments, I practice Jiu Jitsu every week to maintain my health and stay in shape.

My interests extend beyond my field of study such as nutrition, reading, and architecture. Nutrition has been an important aspect of my life at the beginning of COVID-19, since then I've been a vegatarian for nearly three years continuously improving my physical and mental well-being.

Another one of my priorties is reading. Initially, reading did not appeal to me until I realized I hadn't found the right genres that matched my interests. After exploring various subjects, I developed a profound interest in science, social networking, and business literature. Recent reads, such as "Never Eat Alone" by Keith Ferrazzi, "Elon Musk" by Walter Isaacson, and "Atomic Habits" by James Clear, have greatly influenced my perspective and approach to life.

HOBBIES

Being a vegatarian indeed has its limitations, but it also comes with some perks, like cooking! I really enjoy cooking! Not only do I get to make food, but there's also some creativity that takes place. As a vegatarian, it gives me the opportunity to explore new foods and spices, while also maintaining a healthy diet.

TRAVEL
